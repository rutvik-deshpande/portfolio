Sustainable Design
& Technology

I am a 22 year-old Machine Learning Design Engineer at a Singapore based AI-Architecture startup, Digital Blue Foam and also pursuing a Bachelors degree focusing on architecture in India. My main research interest lies in synthesizing artificial intelligence and architectural design, with my work focusing on data-driven design workflows in both urban and architectural scales. I have been working with computational design, machine learning, deep learning, data science, synthetic data generation to design better and sustainable future cities. I, along with the R&D team at DBF, have research papers & abstracts published prestigious global conferences like CAADRIA, Design Modelling Symposium (DMS), and Design Computing and Cognition (DCC). I have also co-led technical workshops at conferences like, CAADRIA and DigitalFutures. I am accredited LEED Green Associate and Kaggle Expert.
